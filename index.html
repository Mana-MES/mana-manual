Powerful Features for Modern Manufacturing

Everything you need to optimize production, track performance, and make data-driven decisions

Real-time OEE

Monitor your factory's health with real-time Overall Equipment Effectiveness tracking. Our system helps you achieve the industry-standard 85% OEE target by providing instant visibility into availability, performance, and quality metrics.

Get daily and shift-by-shift OEE trends, plus insights into top downtime reasons to identify bottlenecks and optimize production flow.

Work Order Management

Streamline your production workflow with comprehensive Work Order (WO) and Operation (OP) management. Create, track, and manage production orders with ease, linking SKUs to production routes and quantities.

Track Work in Progress (WIP) in real-time, monitor daily completion rates, identify defects, and see all active production lines at a glance. Keep your team aligned with current production documents and status updates.

Downtime Tracking

Analyze and eliminate production stoppages with intelligent downtime management. Automatically record machine stops or manually log downtime events with detailed cause tracking.

Visualize downtime patterns with timeline views, categorize stop causes (machine breakdown, material waiting, breaks), and use AI-powered insights to identify recurring issues and optimize your production schedule.
